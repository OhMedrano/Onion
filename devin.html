Devin DollFace Alexis

Devin Dollface Alexis was born and raised in the city of glitter and grime; sometimes her friend, sometimes a waste of rotting time.

In her short, cosmically celestial twenty two years on Earth, Devin has traveled Nation-wide honing her skills and mastering the art of glamour-and-morbid-theatrics all rolled into one; to create the perfect strand of Fashion, Glamour and Special Effects Makeup DNA.

Attaining a major in Film and Television Artistry from MUD-Makeup Designory SoHo in New York City, Devin has also toiled in the study of Esthetics from the Tricoci University of Beauty Culture Chicago.

Devin Dollface Alexis's work ranges from Natural Beauty Application, Basic Men's Makeup Application, Time Period Makeup, Pinup, Glamour, Fashion, Avant Garde Makeup Applications, Face and Body Paint, including Airbrush, Tattoo Coverage and False Eyelash Application.

Devin's work also includes Special Effects, ranging from Character Development, Laying Hair, Old Age Makeup Application, Injuries, Wounds, Burns, as well as Sculpting, Molding and applying various forms of Latex and Silicone Prosthetics, and Basic Lifecasting.

Devin's extensive background in art, starting as a child includes a Major in Comics and Animation from Art and Design High School, a scholarship in Sculpting to Cooper Union in New York City, her own Face Painting Business and Tattooing.

Besides Art, Devin has also worked for various music venues in New York City doing Promotions, writing Press Kits, helping with Graphics, Website and Venue Management; including interning at The Knitting Factory NY as well as Buzz Publicity NY. She interned for Gothic Horror Movie Magazine, Diabolique where she helped to manage the website horrorunlimited.com and wrote an occassional Art Column. Devin also works extensively with Emerging Artist Connect, writing a Makeup column for their online forum, presenting art, modeling and doing makeup for various art gallery events around New York.

With a diverse infusion of collected experience, Devin Dollface Alexis contributes a unique, dynamic and multitonal vision to every endeavor she sets her mind too. Especially when it comes to her ultimate passions: Makeup and Special Effects.
